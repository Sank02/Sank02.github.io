I'm a data professional with ~3 years of Software Engineering experience, specialising in content development and testing for an e-learning platform — including owning team management responsibilities. That grounding gives me something most analysts lack: I understand how data is created, not just consumed.

Over the past 1.5 years, I've made a deliberate, self-directed pivot into data analytics — building a portfolio that spans PostgreSQL, Python, Power BI, Tableau, Alteryx, and Databricks across end-to-end projects in retail, fintech, and publishing domains.

My edge is the bridge between technical depth and business context. I don't just write queries — I frame findings as decisions. I'm drawn to roles where data shapes strategy, not just reports.

Based in the Mumbai / Thane / Navi Mumbai metro. Open to remote and hybrid opportunities across India.
